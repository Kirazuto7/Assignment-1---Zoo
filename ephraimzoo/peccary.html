THE PECCARY

Peccaries are hooved mammals with small ears and short tails.

They are generally omnivorous. Their straight tusks can break open hard seeds.

They have been farm animals and pets, like pigs.

Kind of Peccary Characteristics
Collared Peccary Slender legs, primarily herbivorous
White-lipped Peccary Strong scent glands, brown/black coat
Chacoan Peccary Small feet, developed sinuses
