The Pig

In broad terms, pigs are domesticated hooved mammals with bristly hair, short legs, and curved tails. They are omnivorous, and they use flat snouts to dig through the soil for food. Pigs are typically farmed for their meat, but they have also been kept as pets.

Kind of Pig Characteristics
Duroc Auburn color, quiet temperament
Yorkshire White color, erect ears
Angeln Saddleback Very rare- Large, lop-eared
Tamworth Highly endangered- Elongated head, lined snout
