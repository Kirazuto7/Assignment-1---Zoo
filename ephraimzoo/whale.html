THE WHALE

Whales are marine mammals with smooth skin, flat tails, and flippers. They return to the surface to breathe.

They are carnivorous.

Some have teeth for eating large prey, and others have fibrous baleen for filtering out smaller prey.

Kind of Whale Characteristics

Humpback Whale Long flippers, thick layer of blubber
